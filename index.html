FAQ

Questions?

How does the AI verification work? expand_more
Our AI uses advanced computer vision to analyze your photos in real-time. It can detect environments (gym, outdoors, kitchen), activities (working out, reading, cooking), and even verify specific items in frame. All processing happens instantly.
Can I upload photos from my gallery? expand_more
No. LockedIn requires live photo capture only. Our anti-cheat system detects and blocks gallery uploads, screenshots, and previously taken photos. This ensures complete honesty in your habit tracking.
What happens if I miss a day? expand_more
Your streak resets to zero. No exceptions. This is what makes LockedIn different—there's real consequence to breaking your commitment. But don't worry, you can always start a new streak immediately.
Is my photo data private? expand_more
Absolutely. Your photos are processed by our AI and then immediately deleted. We never store your images on our servers. Your privacy is paramount—we only care about verifying your habits, not collecting your data.
